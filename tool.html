Enablement Diagnostic Studio

A guided diagnostic designed to help identify whether a challenge is primarily a training issue, a readiness issue, a workflow issue, a visibility issue, a governance issue, or some combination of those. This public MVP also collects recipient email, prepares a webhook-ready roadmap payload, and includes a built-in feedback path.

Step 1Define the situation and who is affected.
Step 2Diagnose the system conditions around clarity, support, access, visibility, and governance.
Step 3Generate the output, send roadmap request, and capture feedback.
PurposeChallenge the reflex to default to “more training” before diagnosing the real problem.

Run a guided diagnostic

This tool is strongest when the user describes one real problem, rates the current conditions honestly, and uses the output to identify what should happen before more content is created.
